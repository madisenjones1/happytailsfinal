Welcome to Happy Tails!

At Happy Tails, we believe your pets deserve the best. Our store specializes in healthy, organic pet food for both cats and dogs, inspired by our beloved pitbull Cruze who taught us the importance of nutrition for animals with allergies.

We are proud to offer a wide selection of carefully chosen brands, each meeting our high standards for quality and ingredients. Whether you're shopping for kibble, wet food, treats, or specialty chew toys, you'll find options that align with your pet's dietary needs and your values.

Here at Happy Tails, we strive on having the best ingredients because of the huge changes we see in our pets! If you would like to learn more, please watch this video on the importance of ingredients in pet food.
